## Electronic Components Supplier for Aerospace Projects

Masline follows a rigorous quality management framework aligned with **AS9100**, **ISO 9001**, and **AS9120** standards. Every component undergoes inspection, validation, and verification to ensure it meets aerospace reliability benchmarks.

### Quality Control Practices
1. **Authorized Sourcing:**  
   Components are procured directly from original manufacturers or verified partners to ensure authenticity and eliminate counterfeit risks.

2. **AS9100-Compliant Inspection:**  
   All parts are inspected and documented under AS9100 procedures for aerospace-grade consistency and reliability.

3. **Traceability:**  
   Each component shipment includes full traceability reports, including manufacturer data, lot numbers, and Certificates of Conformance (CoC).

4. **Environmental Testing:**  
   Components are tested for durability under extreme conditions including temperature fluctuations, vibration, and radiation exposure.

By implementing these quality assurance measures, Masline guarantees that aerospace clients receive only the most reliable and traceable electronic components.
